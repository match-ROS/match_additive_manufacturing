Print-While-Drive for robotic large-scale additive manufacturing

Tobias Recker, Lukas Lachmayer, Hauke Heeren, Torge Kolditz, Annika Raatz

Institute of Assembly Technology and Robotics · Leibniz University Hannover

A framework for continuous multi-robot print-while-drive additive manufacturing using coupled trajectory planning, retiming, and closed-loop robot control.
